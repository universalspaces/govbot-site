Section 2

Description of Service

GovBot is a Discord bot designed to facilitate mock-government roleplay communities ("Servers"). It provides tools including:

  • Election management with First-Past-the-Post and Ranked Choice voting
  • Political party creation and membership tracking
  • Legislative bill proposals, co-sponsorship, and voting
  • A judicial system for filing and adjudicating court cases
  • Referendum and citizen initiative systems
  • Impeachment proceedings for officeholders
  • Government office management with term limit enforcement
  • A fictional treasury system with a government balance, citizen wallets, and in-simulation transactions
  • A web dashboard for viewing government data

GovBot is intended solely for fictional, recreational, and educational mock-government roleplay. It is not affiliated with any real government, political party, or political organisation.

Dashboard sessions are stored temporarily and expire after 24 hours of inactivity. You can revoke GovBot's OAuth2 access at any time from your Discord account's Authorized Apps settings.

Privacy Policy

📅 Effective: January 1, 2025 🔄 Last Updated: March 2026 📋 Version 1.0

Plain English Summary: GovBot stores the minimum data needed to run your mock government — Discord user IDs, server IDs, and the content you create (bills, votes, party names, etc.). We don't sell your data, don't run ads, and you can request deletion at any time.

Section 1

Who We Are

GovBot is a Discord bot developed and operated as an independent project. This Privacy Policy explains what data GovBot collects, how it is used, and your rights regarding that data. By using GovBot you agree to this policy.

Section 2

Data We Collect

GovBot stores only the data that is necessary to operate its government simulation features. We do not collect names, email addresses, IP addresses, or any data beyond what Discord provides during normal bot interactions.

Data Type What it is Why we store it
Discord User ID Your unique Discord user identifier (a number) To associate citizens, votes, bills, cases, and other government actions with a user
Discord Server ID Your server's unique Discord identifier To separate each server's government data and configuration
Government Content Bill text, platform statements, court rulings, constitutional articles, party descriptions, initiative text, charges, etc. To operate the government simulation — this is content you voluntarily submit
Vote Records The fact that you voted, and your choice (for bills, elections, referendums, etc.) To prevent double-voting and to tally results accurately
Treasury & Wallet Balances Your in-simulation wallet balance and a log of transactions you are involved in (amounts, descriptions, who authorized them) To operate the fictional treasury system — balances and transactions have no real-world monetary value
Activity Log A record of government actions (who proposed a bill, who filed a case, etc.) To provide transparency and an audit trail within the server
Admin Audit Log A record of administrative actions taken by server admins (dismissals, announcements, data changes) To provide accountability for administrative actions within the server
Dashboard Session A temporary session token after OAuth2 login; your Discord username and avatar URL To keep you authenticated on the dashboard for up to 24 hours
Reminder Preferences Your user ID, election ID, and requested reminder time To send you a DM reminder only if you explicitly opted in with /remind set
Section 3

Data We Do Not Collect

GovBot does not collect, store, or process:

  • Your real name, email address, or any personally identifiable information beyond your Discord user ID
  • The content of messages you send in Discord channels (GovBot only reads slash command interactions)
  • Your IP address or device information
  • Any data from servers that GovBot is not a member of
  • Real payment information, banking details, or any real-world financial data of any kind — in-simulation wallet balances are fictional numbers with no monetary value
Section 4

How We Use Your Data

All data collected by GovBot is used exclusively to operate the government simulation features within your Discord server. Specifically:

  • Your user ID is used to identify you as a citizen, party member, officeholder, voter, or bill sponsor within your server's government
  • Government content you submit is stored and displayed to members of your server as part of the simulation
  • Vote records are used to enforce one-vote-per-person rules and to calculate results
  • The activity log is used to provide a transparent record of government actions visible to your server's members
  • Reminder data is used solely to send you a single DM you explicitly requested

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, sale to third parties, or any purpose outside of operating GovBot's stated features.

Section 5

Data Storage and Security

GovBot stores all server data in a SQLite database hosted on the same server as the bot. Data is not shared across servers — each server's government data is fully isolated and only accessible to members of that server through the bot's commands and dashboard.

Dashboard authentication uses Discord's official OAuth2 system. We do not store your Discord password or token. Session data is stored server-side and expires automatically.

While we take reasonable precautions to protect stored data, no system is perfectly secure. We cannot guarantee absolute protection against unauthorised access.

Section 6

Data Sharing and Third Parties

GovBot does not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties. The only external service involved is Discord, through whose platform all interactions take place. Discord's own Privacy Policy governs the data Discord collects about you.

GovBot's dashboard is served as a standalone web application. It does not load any third-party advertising, tracking scripts, or analytics services.

Section 7

Data Retention

GovBot retains server data for as long as the bot remains in your server. Specific retention rules:

Data Retention
Server configuration & government data Retained while GovBot is in the server; deleted on request after removal
Closed elections, passed laws, concluded cases Retained indefinitely as a historical record unless deletion is requested
Treasury balances & transaction history Retained while GovBot is in the server; deleted on request. Wallet balances reset to zero if a citizen is de-registered
Dashboard session tokens Expire automatically after 24 hours of inactivity
Sent reminder records Marked as sent and retained briefly for deduplication, then ignored
Activity logs Retained as part of the server's government history; the most recent 50 entries are displayed
Section 8

Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your data stored by GovBot:

  • Access: You can view all government data associated with your user ID using the bot's info and profile commands (e.g. /citizen profile, /stats member)
  • Deletion: You may request deletion of your personal data from GovBot's database by contacting us through the support server. Note that some data (such as vote tallies and bill records) is aggregated and cannot be individually removed without corrupting the historical record
  • Correction: If incorrect data has been stored due to a bug, contact us and we will investigate
  • Opt-out of reminders: Use /remind cancel at any time to stop a pending DM reminder
  • Revoke dashboard access: Remove GovBot's OAuth2 authorisation from Discord's Authorised Apps settings at any time

Note for EU/EEA residents: If you are located in the European Economic Area, you may have additional rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), including the right to data portability and to lodge a complaint with your local supervisory authority. Please contact us to exercise these rights.
