Features

What GovBot does

🗳️
Elections
FPTP and Ranked Choice (instant-runoff). Scheduled starts, candidate registration, auto-tally, winner announcement.
📊
Referendums & Polls
Yes/no referendums and multi-option informal polls. Auto-close at deadline, changeable votes, anonymous mode.
📣
Citizen Initiatives
Citizens collect signatures to demand government action. Configurable thresholds, auto-announces when fulfilled.
⚖️
Impeachment
File charges, hold trials, vote to convict or acquit. Convicted officials are automatically removed from office.
📋
Recall Petitions
Citizens petition to trigger a by-election against a sitting officeholder. Signature-driven with configurable thresholds.
🏛️
Political Parties
Found parties, manage members, promote officers, transfer leadership. Automatic succession when a leader leaves.
📜
Legislature
Propose bills, amend, co-sponsor, vote, pass into law or reject. Parliament role enforcement. Bill deadlines and quorum.
💰
Treasury
Government balance, citizen wallets, full transaction ledger, grants, fines, salary payments, and peer transfers.
⚖️
Judiciary
Appointed judges, file cases, assign and rule, court appeals. Original judge cannot rule on their own appeal.
💼
Offices & Term Limits
Create positions, appoint or remove holders, sync Discord roles, enforce per-office term limits, track full history.
📖
Constitution
Ratify and repeal constitutional articles. Full version history stored.
🔧
Admin Tools
Audit log, announcements, bulk data management, citizenship gates, server health stats, and a web dashboard.
