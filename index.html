GovBot

A full-featured Discord bot for mock-government roleplay communities. Elections, legislature, judiciary, treasury, and more.

Add to Discord Terms & Privacy
What it does

Features

🗳️
Elections
First-Past-the-Post and Ranked Choice (instant-runoff). Scheduled starts, candidate registration, auto-tally, winner announcement.
📊
Referendums
Put yes/no questions directly to all citizens. Auto-closes at deadline with result announcement.
📣
Citizen Initiatives
Citizens collect signatures to demand government action. Configurable thresholds, auto-announces when fulfilled.
⚖️
Impeachment
File charges, hold trials, vote to convict or acquit. Convicted officials are automatically removed from office.
🏛️
Political Parties
Found parties, manage members, promote officers, transfer leadership. Automatic succession when a leader leaves.
📜
Legislature
Propose bills, amend them, co-sponsor, vote (changeable while open), pass into law or reject, repeal laws.
💰
Treasury
Government balance, citizen wallets, full transaction ledger, grants, fines, salary payments, and citizen-to-citizen transfers.
📋
Term Limits
Per-office term limits enforced automatically at election registration and office appointment. Full term history tracked.
⚖️
Judiciary
File court cases, assign judges, issue verdicts with full rulings. Complete court docket.
💼
Offices
Create government positions, appoint or remove holders, sync Discord roles, track term history.
📖
Constitution
Ratify and repeal constitutional articles. Full version history.
🔧
Admin Tools
Audit log, government announcements, bulk data management, server health stats, and more.
